Executa GTM Plan:
5 Pillars · 10-Day Launch · 12-Week Roadmap

A systematic plan to take Executa from founder-led sales to a repeatable acquisition channel reaching 200 boutique M&A advisory and mid-market PE firms across the UK and Europe.

GTM Pillars

Five Pillars of the Acquisition System

Each pillar represents a distinct acquisition mechanism. Pillar 1 and 2 are activated first — they create the pipeline that feeds pillars 3, 4, and 5.

01

PE Deal Team Mapping

200 verified UK and European mid-market PE funds (£200M to £1B AUM) with named Partners and Investment Directors. Buying signal: analyst hiring (proxy for live deal flow). ICP score per firm.

Primary ICP · Activate First
02

M&A Boutique Advisor Channel

Rothschild tier B and below: boutique advisory firms with 5 to 50 professionals running 10 to 30 mandates per year. Decision-maker: MD or Partner who personally writes IC memos.

Secondary ICP · Activate Week 2
03

Fund Close Timing Trigger

PE firms 12 to 18 months into a new fund are in peak deal sourcing mode. Companies House confirmation statement filing dates proxy for fund vintage and sourcing calendar. These firms are in their highest-velocity deal window.

Signal Trigger · Companies House
04

Analyst Replacement ROI Channel

Firms that recently posted or hired a junior analyst for deal screening are experiencing exactly the pain Executa solves. Job board activity (LinkedIn, Reed, eFinancialCareers) is the signal. These firms are the warmest entry point in the target universe.

Timing Signal · Job Board Data
05

Content and Authority Engine

Mathew's LinkedIn profile becomes the trust-building channel for PE Partners and M&A MDs over 18 months. 2 to 3 posts per week on deal workflow intelligence. 20-week value-only nurture sequence. The Authority Engine converts the 95 percent not-ready market into a 24-month pipeline.

Authority Engine Score: 6/7 · RECOMMENDED
10-Day Launch Sequence

Days 1 to 10 — What Happens and When

The critical first 10 days from programme launch to first qualified conversations. Every day has a specific deliverable and outcome.

Day Activity Channel Output
Day 1 ICP definition and segment scoring. Confirm primary ICP (mid-market PE) and secondary ICP (boutique M&A advisory). Map buying signal criteria per segment. Prep ICP scorecard. Segment definitions. Signal criteria list.
Day 2 Start account list build — Tier 1 (boutique M&A advisory). Pull UK advisory firms from Companies House + LinkedIn. Verify MD and Partner contacts. Score against ICP criteria. Research Tier 1 draft: 80 to 100 firms with contact data.
Day 3 Account list build — Tier 2 (mid-market PE). Pull UK PE funds from Preqin signals + LinkedIn. Identify analyst hiring firms (job board scan). Score and rank by ICP fit. Research Tier 2 draft: 80 to 100 firms. Combined list: 200 firms.
Day 4 Sequence build — Email 1 (Trigger angle). Personalise to Tier 1 segment: boutique advisory founder-led sales ceiling frame. Review and finalise. PSS check (target 85+). Email Email 1 final. Subject: Jefferies. Lazard. Rothschild.
Day 5 Sequence build — Email 2 (Friction angle). Mid-market gap frame. Rogo Series D context. Urgency: 12-month evaluation window. LinkedIn sequence (connect, follow-up, breakup). EmailLinkedIn Email 2 final. LinkedIn 3-step sequence.
Day 6 HubSpot setup. Pipeline stages configured: Cold → Replied → Demo Booked → Demo Done → Pilot Signed. Lead import from verified contact list. Sequence integration. CRM HubSpot pipeline live. 200 contacts imported.
Day 7 Deliverability check. SPF, DKIM, DMARC verified. Domain warm-up status checked. Spam word audit on all emails. Test sends to seed inboxes. Email Deliverability report. Green light or action items.
Day 8 Review call with Mathew. Walk through account list, sequences, and HubSpot setup. Approve or adjust. Confirm sending schedule and daily volume (target: 10 to 20 per day). Review Final approval. Launch ready.
Day 9 Launch Tier 1 wave (boutique M&A advisory). First 20 firms. Email 1 sent. LinkedIn connect requests sent in parallel. Monitor for early replies. EmailLinkedIn Wave 1 live. 20 firms contacted. Monitoring active.
Day 10 First reply scan. Qualify early replies (genuine intent vs polite curiosity vs objection). Flag warm prospects for Mathew. Launch Tier 2 wave (mid-market PE) in parallel. EmailQA First warm prospects identified. Tier 2 wave launched.
12-Week Roadmap

Month by Month — What Gets Built and What Gets Measured

Three 30-day phases. Each phase builds on the one before. At the end of 90 days, Executa has a working acquisition system the sales hire can run independently.

Month 1 · Weeks 1–4

Foundation and Launch

  • 200-firm account list completed and verified
  • Email sequences (Trigger + Friction) live
  • LinkedIn sequence active on Mathew's profile
  • HubSpot pipeline configured and populated
  • First 60 to 80 firms contacted
  • First 5 to 10 replies received and qualified
  • 2 to 3 demos booked from first wave
  • Authority Engine content calendar drafted (if approved)
Month 2 · Weeks 5–8

Scale and Optimise

  • Wave 2: Next 80 firms contacted
  • Winning subject line and opening variant identified from reply data
  • ICP refined based on who is replying (boutique vs PE, deal size)
  • 10 to 20 qualified conversations in progress
  • 5 to 10 live demos with PE Partners and M&A MDs
  • First Authority Engine posts live on Mathew's LinkedIn (if approved)
  • Reference call programme activated from existing client base
  • Weekly pipeline report to Mathew and co-founders
Month 3 · Weeks 9–12

Convert and Systematise

  • Wave 3: Final 40 to 60 firms contacted
  • 3 to 5 signed pilots from outbound programme
  • First named case study drafted from new client cohort
  • Referral trigger sequence activated (30-day post-onboarding)
  • Sales hire handover: full system documentation
  • HubSpot training for sales hire and co-founders
  • SuperReturn 2026 (June) pipeline identified for conference follow-up
  • 90-day retrospective and ICP refinement session
